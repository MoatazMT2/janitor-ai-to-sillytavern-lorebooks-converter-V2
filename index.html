How to copy the lorebook:

Works only if the bot creator made the lorebook Public.

  2. Open the desired bot on Janitor.ai
  3. In the more section, find Lorebook
  4. On the opened page, click View Script
  5. Click Clone script
  6. Click on the icon
  7. Click on the copy icon
  8. Paste copied text into the field below and Convert.
